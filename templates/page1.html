Meet Ti, Chicago 311 Service Request Virtual Agent

Now we know it may be tempting to be like Scranton and put those pesky potholes to good use, and Ti loves Jim Bachor as much as the next virtual agent, but we hope you'll contribute to a safer, more user-friendly Chicago by reporting your 311 requests here.

Ti takes your service request and passes it along to the city to be actioned. He also has information on historical resolution times, and will soon be able to notify you once your request has been actioned. To start, just click on the blue button to your right!
