Meet Ti, the Chicago 311 Service Request ButlerBot

Now Ti loves Jim Bachor as much as the next chatbot, and we know it may be tempting to be like Scranton and put those pesky potholes to good use, but we hope you'll contribute to a safer, more user-friendly Chicago by reporting your 311 requests here!

Ti can takes your service request and passes it along to the city to be actioned. He also has information on historical resolution times, and can notify you once your request has been actioned.

To start, just click on the big blue button on the right hand corner of your screen!
